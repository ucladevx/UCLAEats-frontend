Find Food Any Day of the Week

Our app shows the menu for any restaurant or dining hall for the next seven days, enabling you to plan you meals in advance, anywhere.

Other apps stop functioning and aren't supported. We guarantee support as long as UCLA's DevX organization is around. Just enjoy your food!

Find a meal you love! Tag it in the app, and we'll notify you when it appears on the menu again. You'll never miss that California Burrito at Rende again.

Don't Eat Alone

Ever feel lonely at a dining hall? Now, there's no reason to ever eat alone. You can message anybody to find a person to eat with, or allow us to match you with someone of common interests! Enjoying a meal with company is always better than eating a meal alone.
